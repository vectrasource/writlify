Privacy Policy

We built Writlify to be as private as possible. Here's exactly what we do — and don't — collect.

💡 Short version: Writlify does not require you to create an account. We do not sell your data. The text you paste into our tools is sent to the AI API to generate results and is not stored by us.

01

Overview

Writlify ("we", "us", or "our") is a free AI writing toolkit operated by Vectrasource Digital Suite. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at writlify.com.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our tools are designed to be used without creating an account, and we intentionally minimise the data we collect.

02

Data we collect

Usage data

We may collect basic, anonymised usage data such as pages visited, browser type, device type, and general geographic region (country level). This is used solely to understand how the product is being used and to improve it.

Rate limiting data

To prevent abuse, we store a daily usage counter in your browser's localStorage. This is stored locally on your device only — it never leaves your browser and we cannot access it.

Text you submit to tools

When you use an AI-powered tool, the text you enter is sent to our API endpoint and forwarded to the Claude AI API (by Anthropic) to generate a response. We do not log, store, or retain this text on our servers.

03

Data we don't collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Account credentials (there are no accounts)
  • Payment information (the service is free)
  • The content of your tool inputs after processing is complete
  • Any data from children under 13
04

How we use data

Any data we collect is used only for:

  • Operating the service — processing your tool requests via the AI API
  • Improving the product — understanding which tools are used most
  • Preventing abuse — enforcing daily usage limits
  • Analytics — understanding traffic patterns at an aggregate, anonymised level

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

05

Third parties

Anthropic (Claude AI)

AI-powered tools send your input text to Anthropic's Claude API to generate results. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle API data. You can read it at anthropic.com/privacy.

Vercel

Writlify is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, request metadata) as part of infrastructure operations. See Vercel's privacy policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Google Fonts

We load fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log requests for fonts. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

06

Cookies & local storage

Writlify does not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage solely to store your daily tool usage count for rate limiting purposes. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is automatically cleared after 24 hours
  • Contains no personal information
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data
07

Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data linked to your identity, there is very little data to request, correct, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Ask what data (if any) we hold about you
  • Request deletion of any data we may hold
  • Opt out of any analytics tracking

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

08

Children's privacy

Writlify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used our service and provided personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Writlify after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
