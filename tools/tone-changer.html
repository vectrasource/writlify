FAQ

Common questions.

What tones can I choose from?
The tool offers six tones: Professional, Casual & friendly, Confident & bold, Empathetic, Witty, and Persuasive — covering most writing situations from business emails to social posts.
Does it change the meaning of my text?
No, it keeps the core message while adjusting word choice, sentence structure, and style to match the selected tone. The meaning stays the same — the voice changes.
Is this useful for business emails?
Yes, it's especially helpful for making casual drafts sound more professional, or for softening an overly formal message into something warmer and more approachable.
