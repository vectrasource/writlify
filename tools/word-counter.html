FAQ

Common questions.

What does this tool count?
Words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, estimated reading time (at 238 words/min), and estimated speaking time (at 130 words/min) — all updated in real time.
Does it update in real time?
Yes, counts update instantly as you type or paste text — no button to press, no page reload. Everything runs locally in your browser.
Is there a text length limit?
No, paste text of any length. The counter handles everything from a short tweet to a multi-chapter document without slowing down.
Is this useful for social media posts?
Yes, it helps you stay within character limits for platforms like Twitter/X (280 characters), LinkedIn posts, Instagram captions, and more.
